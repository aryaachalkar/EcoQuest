The Mission Brief
The Mula-Mutha river is vital to Pune's ecosystem, but plastic waste is suffocating the banks. Pune Rivers Trust needs students across the city to participate in localized clean-up drives.

Your mission is to visit a local green space or riverbank, collect at least 1kg of solid waste, and correctly segregate the plastics before submitting them to a recyclying center.
