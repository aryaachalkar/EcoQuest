April Task — Gudi Padwa
Make an Eco-Friendly Gudi
Festival: Gudi Padwa (Maharashtra New Year)
Traditionally, a Gudi is made using a silk cloth on a bamboo stick. This year, instead of buying a new synthetic cloth, use old sarees or cotton dupatta from home. Make your own natural dye using turmeric and hibiscus flowers, and learn how natural pigments work (Chemistry Ch. 4 — Acids, Bases & Salts).
