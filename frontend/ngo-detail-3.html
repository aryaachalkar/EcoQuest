The Mission Brief
Healthy forests are our lifeline. Tree Friends Maharashtra is building a real-time tracking map of local native tree species.

Your mission is to explore your local green space or street. Identify 5 native trees, determine their scientific and local names, and hand-make small tags for them. Take photos of your tags tied to the trees!
